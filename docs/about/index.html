What makes me different ?

I was born with a defect that after meeting a person or even knowing a person a fairly long time, I can't perfectly reconstruct their face. But I can still recognize them in a crowd. This structural difference enable me to appreciate the beauty of blurriness. By blurriness, I mean I get to awe at something without digging into the details. For engineers, the beauty originated from the understanding of the minutiae, the enlightenment that happens when the details are revealed. But, for me, the beauty can also lay on the fact of not revealing the details, just let the incredible engineering work stay there and I get to appreciate it all day. Therefore, I'm more interested in architectural designs and systems, and able to generate unique perspectives towards architectures.
